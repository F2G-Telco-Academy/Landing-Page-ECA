## ðŸ“Š **HERO MESSAGE FORMULAS**

### **Formula 1: Value + Qualifier**

**Pattern**: [Quality Descriptor] + [Product Category] + [Key Differentiator]

**Examples**:
- "Professional Cellular Analysis | Made Simple"
- "Enterprise Network Testing | Zero Cost"
- "Real-Time Monitoring | Real Results"

**Use when**: You want to emphasize quality + accessibility

---

### **Formula 2: Problem + Solution**

**Pattern**: [Pain Point] + [Solution Statement]

**Examples**:
- "Stop Paying $5,000 | For Network Testing Tools"
- "One Device Isn't Enough | Test Four Simultaneously"
- "Tired of Expensive Tools? | Here's Your Free Alternative"

**Use when**: Target audience has clear pain point

---

### **Formula 3: Comparison + Benefit**

**Pattern**: [What You Know] + [What We Offer Instead]

**Examples**:
- "Everything QXDM Does | Plus Features It Doesn't"
- "Professional Features | Without Vendor Lock-In"
- "What $5,000 Software Does | For Free"

**Use when**: Competitive positioning is key

---

### **Formula 4: Audience + Promise**

**Pattern**: [Target Audience] + [Outcome Promise]

**Examples**:
- "Built by Engineers | For Engineers"
- "Network Analysis | For Every Budget"
- "For RF Professionals | Who Refuse to Overpay"

**Use when**: Community or audience-specific focus
