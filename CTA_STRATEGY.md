## ðŸŽ¨ **CTA DESIGN PRINCIPLES**

### **Visual Hierarchy**

**Primary CTAs**:
- Large size (min 50px height)
- High contrast (primary color)
- White text, bold font
- Drop shadow for depth
- Icon if appropriate
- Hover: Scale 1.05, darker color

**Secondary CTAs**:
- Medium size (40px height)
- Outline style or lighter color
- Less contrast than primary
- Hover: Fill background

**Tertiary CTAs**:
- Text links or small buttons
- Gray or secondary color
- Underline on hover
- No background

---

### **Button Copy Rules**

**DO**:
- âœ… Use action verbs: Download, Get, Start, Try
- âœ… Be specific: "Download Free for Windows"
- âœ… Indicate benefit: "Start Testing in 5 Minutes"
- âœ… Remove friction: "No Signup Required"
- âœ… Create urgency: "Download Now"

**DON'T**:
- âŒ Generic: "Click Here", "Submit"
- âŒ Vague: "Learn More" (what will I learn?)
- âŒ Passive: "Information Available"
- âŒ Long: "Click here to download the free version"

---

### **Color Strategy**

**Primary CTA**: 
- Background: Primary blue (#1e40af)
- Text: White (#ffffff)
- Hover: Darker blue (#1e3a8a)
- Shadow: rgba(30, 64, 175, 0.3)

**Secondary CTA**:
- Border: Primary blue (#1e40af)
- Text: Primary blue (#1e40af)
- Background: Transparent
- Hover: Background primary blue, text white

**Tertiary CTA**:
- Text: Gray (#64748b)
- Hover: Primary blue (#1e40af)
- Underline on hover
